A Burla demo · data-first linguistic archaeology

The loneliest paper in science is about Norwegian corporate tax filings.

We embedded every single arXiv abstract ever posted — 2,710,783 of them — with MiniLM-L6-v2, clustered them into 400 topics, and let the geometry surface three things without asking: the topics that collapsed, the topics that appeared overnight, and the one paper that sits furthest from anything anyone else has ever written.

Headline
arXiv:2203.12842 — "Financial statements of companies in Norway".
Its 5th-nearest neighbor across 2.71 M papers has cosine similarity 0.138. No other paper on arXiv is remotely about the same thing.
