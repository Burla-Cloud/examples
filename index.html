An Etruscan bronze and a Greek ceramic, 49 centuries apart, are visually near-identical.

We fetched every Met Museum CC0 artwork with a published image, CLIP-embedded all 191,922 of them, and searched the 512-dimensional space for pairs that look the same despite being separated by thousands of years and half the planet. No one at the Met ever filed these two together.
